Session 2 — Core Products
GLP-1 Weight Loss
Semaglutide · Tirzepatide · Ondansetron
⚠ Important — Compliance

This class of drugs is highly scrutinized right now due to ongoing lawsuits with big pharma. It is very important we do not reference the commercial drugs when explaining these to providers. We are not a "copycat" drug. Our products are compounded with either B12, B6, or Glycine in addition to the active ingredient. They require a written reason for compounding by the provider in order for us to stay compliant. This has never happened in the history of compounding.

Greenlist Suppliers

The FDA has a requirement for 503A compounding pharmacies to use a "green list" supplier to source the API for these products. We already use FDA approved vendors, but not all FDA approved vendors are "green list" suppliers. Make sure we let customers know we are adhering to using "green list" suppliers for sourcing these products. Not all pharmacies are doing it now, but there is a good chance the FDA cracks down on this in the future.

Products
Semaglutide
Gen 1 GLP · Commercial alternatives: Wegovy and Ozempic (Novo)
The product that started the GLP craze. Works almost as well as tirzepatide, just more side effects — mainly nausea. There are studies that say GLPs cause muscle loss — this is a little misleading. When calorie consumption goes down so does protein consumption. It is important for patients to engage in resistance training and meet protein goals while on GLPs to preserve lean mass. Resistance training also helps preserve bone density.
Tirzepatide
Gen 2 GLP · Commercial alternatives: Mounjaro and Zepbound (Lilly)
The second GLP product to follow up semaglutide. Touted as a superior product mainly due to decreased side effects and an increase in weight loss. It is the more expensive option currently.
Ondansetron
Anti-nausea
May be something we carry that some providers ask for to help the patient with nausea when they start a GLP. Not a money maker.
Dosing — Titration Schedule
GLP drugs use a titration schedule — the patient starts at a lower dose and the dose is increased, typically monthly, until maximum weight loss with the least amount of side effects is reached
Sometimes patients stall so the dose is slightly increased to begin weight loss again
Semaglutide: Starting dose: 0.25–0.5mg per week / Max dose: 2.5mg per week
Tirzepatide: Starting dose: 2.5mg per week / Max dose: 15mg per week
Customers: Wellness, Med Spas, Family and Internal Medicine, etc.
Semaglutide and tirzepatide are peptides. We keep them separate from the peptides section because these two products are currently the biggest moving products we make.

TRT / HRT / Anabolics
Full formulary — controlled substance compliance required
⚠ Important — Controlled Substances

This class of drugs are controlled substances. It requires us to have the provider's DEA license on file. It also requires a wet signature or some form of electronic verification via 2FA or a physical token issued to the provider in order for us to be compliant. We can also compliantly accept a "verbal" from the provider, but not something we want to do long term because it is disruptive to operations.

Testosterone Cypionate — Overview

Patient range is younger adults to older aged men. Low dose testosterone is also becoming increasingly more prescribed to women. Testosterone is compounded in an oil base, usually of cottonseed oil, grape seed oil, or more often now MCT oil. Cottonseed has been around the longest but causes the injection site reactions — it is also the most viscous, so takes longer to draw up and you have to dose IM. Grape seed and MCT are less viscous so you dose with a much smaller syringe SubQ or shallow IM.

Formulations
Testosterone Cypionate (Men)
200mg/ml · 5ml and 10ml vials · IM or shallow IM
Most commonly seen for TRT for men. Dosed anywhere from once weekly to daily, but 3x a week is most common. Usually IM or shallow IM.
GSO & MCT Oil Base
Formulation note
We will be making our testosterone in GSO or MCT. MCT is gaining popularity but is more expensive currently. Evidence points to it being superior to GSO as it is less inflammatory. We will start with GSO but may add an option for MCT or switch completely if we can source it at close to the same cost.
Testosterone Margins
Important pricing note
Testosterone is one of the most commonly prescribed sterile products we make. It is steady business but the margins can be very low with clinic bill accounts. It is also very labor intensive to make. We want to sell it because it helps diversify us but we don't want to go too low on pricing to get the business. Patient Pay clinics like Urology, OBGYN, etc. is usually best for this product because we can actually make a decent margin. They also prescribe a lot of it.
Testosterone Cypionate / Propionate Blend
160mg/40mg/ml
A less commonly used formulation for TRT, but clinics that use this typically do a lot of it. The propionate acts instantly and keeps levels high in between injections so the patient doesn't experience a dip in energy. The downside is propionate has a higher rate of injection site reactions, but at a smaller dose you don't see it as much.
Enanthate
Medium half-life
You may come across clinics that like to use enanthate. As of right now we don't plan on making it due to it not being common, but if a clinic does good volume let's have a conversation and see if it makes sense to add to the formulary.
Nandrolone Decanoate
200mg/ml · 10ml vials · "Deca-Durabolin" · Wellness · Urology
An anabolic steroid typically prescribed for bodybuilders or patients with an underlying condition causing extremely low muscle mass. Not super commonly prescribed but some clinics see a lot of bodybuilders so it is a good offering to have.
Stanozolol
25mg and 50mg capsules · "Winstrol" · Wellness · Urology · Men's Health
Another anabolic steroid prescribed less often. An oral capsule offering. You won't see this requested often, but it is an offering for the bodybuilder crowd.
Oxandrolone (Troche)
20mg and 50mg · "Anavar" / "Oxandrin"
An oral sublingual anabolic. Another one for the bodybuilding crowd but there are more providers using it in a low dose. Some providers prefer it in troche form because it is thought to bypass being metabolized in the liver. Troches have 2 score lines to quarter the troche, so it works for patients taking a full dose or low dose.
Testosterone Cream
Multiple strengths · Topiclick applicator · "Androgel"
Topical TRT. Has been around a long time. Does not work as well as injectable but can get patients to a reasonable testosterone level with decent clinical outcomes. Not a great option for someone with children because the cream can transfer with skin-to-skin contact. Each click gives 1/4 dose of the strength.
Enclomiphene
12.5mg, 25mg, 50mg capsules · NOT a controlled substance
An isomer of clomiphene that has become very popular over the last few years. No commercially available product. Used as an alternative to TRT to treat secondary hypogonadism. Superior to clomiphene citrate because it does not increase estradiol. A SERM — works by blocking estrogen receptors in the brain to increase FSH and LH, which signal the testes to increase sperm and testosterone production. Great alternative for men wanting to preserve fertility and testicular function. Can get most men to overall testosterone levels of 750–900 while maintaining a natural estrogen balance.
Progesterone
50mg, 75mg, 100mg, 150mg SR capsules · HRT · Wellness · OBGYN · Women's Health
An HRT product primarily prescribed to women. Used to treat perimenopausal/menopausal symptoms (hot flashes, insomnia), amenorrhea, and to protect the uterus from endometrial thickening when taking estrogen therapy. Natural micronized progesterone offers improved safety profiles for sleep and mood.
Thyroid (T3/T4)
Capsules · Porcine or plant-derived · "Armour Thyroid"
Commercial product is Armour Thyroid — derived from desiccated porcine thyroid. Combination of T3 and T4. T4 has a longer half-life for more consistent levels; T3 is faster acting. Most commonly prescribed for hypothyroidism. We will also offer a T3/T4 formulation that is plant-derived and can be tailored to better match the amount of T3/T4 the provider wants.

Tadalafil
6mg, 12mg, 24mg · Mint chewable tablets and/or capsules · "Cialis" (Lilly)
PDE5 inhibitor to treat ED. Can be taken daily or higher dose as needed. 36-hour half-life. Low-dose daily has cardiovascular benefits and helps prevent atherosclerosis. Most clinics prescribe this. Lower margins, but easy to make and often prescribed with other products we make.
Sildenafil
50mg, 100mg SR capsules and Troches · "Viagra" (Pfizer)
PDE5 inhibitor to treat ED. Taken as needed before sexual activity, usually lasts 4–6 hours. Patient must avoid nitrates and not take more than once daily. Priapism is low risk — typically only occurs when mixing medications or taking more than prescribed. Troches work a little faster, which is the benefit of troche vs capsule.
Trimix
Sterile injectable · Urology · Refrigerated — cold chain
Typically used in older patients or those with trauma requiring a stronger medication. Injected directly into the corpus cavernosum. Must be refrigerated. Active ingredients: Alprostadil (dilates blood vessels, relaxes smooth muscle), Papaverine (vasodilator), Phentolamine (blocks epinephrine and norepinephrine to prevent vasoconstriction).
Super Trimix
Sterile injectable · Urology · Refrigerated — cold chain
Another variation of Trimix that has twice the amount of Phentolamine.
Quad Mix
Sterile injectable · Urology — severe cases · Also: Super Quad Mix
Stronger version of Trimix with a fourth active ingredient: Atropine — inhibits nerve impulses responsible for smooth muscle contraction. Typically given for very severe cases of ED. There is also a Super Quad Mix. We will not start with this on the formulary but can make it if we see a need for it.
Scream Cream
Compounded topical cream/gel · Urology · Wellness
Used for female arousal to increase blood flow to the clitoris. Main active ingredients are sildenafil and sometimes low-dose testosterone. This used to be popular but I'm not sure what the demand is anymore. Peptides
Current market overview & full formulary — 17 products
Current Peptide Market
2 Years Ago
Peptides were mainly made by 503A compounding pharmacies.
Late 2023
FDA put 19 of the most popular peptides on the "Category 2" list and pharmacies have not been legally allowed to compound them since. Some pharmacies are still doing it but if they were to get audited by the FDA they would face extremely punishing fines and possibly being shut down.
Today
This ruling created a black market for "Research Only" peptides. These peptides are typically being imported from China premade and are basically under the same jurisdiction as supplements — meaning the customer does not know what they are actually injecting.
July 2026 & Early 2027
RFK Jr. is a big proponent for peptides and is looking to reverse this decision for 14 of these peptides. They will be reviewed in July 2026 and at the beginning of 2027 to remove them from Category 2 and allow 503A compounding pharmacies to make them again. They have realized how much the black market has grown and want to have regulations and quality standards for patients that want to use them.
Full Formulary — in Excel Order
PT-141 Nasal Spray
Nasal spray · Urology · Wellness
About / Clinical Benefits

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) nasal spray is an off-label, compounded peptide used to treat low libido and sexual dysfunction in both men and women. It works directly on the central nervous system to stimulate sexual desire in the brain, rather than just increasing blood flow.

How It Works

Unlike traditional ED medications (like Viagra/Cialis), PT-141 activates melanocortin receptors in the brain. By stimulating these neurochemical pathways, it boosts sexual motivation and arousal, and can also facilitate erectile responses in men.

Additional Information

Delivered as a nasal spray. Typically one to two sprays per nostril 45–60 minutes before desired sexual activity.

NAD+ (Injectable)
Injectable · Longevity · Wellness
About / Clinical Benefits

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a vital coenzyme found in all living cells, essential for energy production, DNA repair, and cell signaling. Because NAD+ levels naturally decline with age, boosting them has become popular for promoting cellular health and longevity.

How It Works

Energy Production: NAD shuttles electrons between molecules in the mitochondria to convert nutrients into cellular energy (ATP). Cellular Repair: NAD+ serves as necessary fuel for specific longevity proteins such as sirtuins and PARPs. Sirtuins help regulate metabolic health and inflammation, while PARPs seek out and repair damaged DNA.

Additional Information

NAD+ is technically not a peptide but it is grouped with the peptides because it is commonly prescribed with other peptides.

AOD 9604 (Troche)
Sublingual troche · Weight Loss · Wellness
About / Clinical Benefits

AOD 9604 troche is a sublingual lozenge containing AOD 9604, a modified peptide fragment of human growth hormone. It is designed to target stubborn fat metabolism and promote weight loss.

How It Works

Acts on metabolic pathways to stimulate fat breakdown and limit fat storage. The troche advantage: dissolving under the tongue allows the peptide to absorb directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive tract and liver and avoiding the need for daily injections.

Additional Information

Also available as an injectable. Currently not approved to compound as it is on the FDA's Category 2 list in injectable form. AOD targets the largest adipocytes in the body, which is typically visceral and abdominal fat.

BPC-157 (Injectable)
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness · Sports Med
About / Clinical Benefits

Primarily used for soft-tissue regeneration, including tendon/ligament healing, muscle regeneration, and potential gut health support.

How It Works

Promotes angiogenesis — especially important for healing tendons and ligaments which have poor blood supply. Increases growth hormone receptors on tendon fibroblasts, making tissue more responsive to natural growth factors. Reduces pro-inflammatory cytokines. Originally studied for ulcers — helps strengthen and repair the mucosal lining of the gut and supports nerve regeneration.

Additional Information

One of the most popular peptides. Ortho may have strong interest in this peptide.

BPC-157 (Capsules)
Oral capsules · GI · Wellness
About / Clinical Benefits

Body Protective Compound 157. Supports gastrointestinal health, tissue repair, and inflammation management.

How It Works

Reduces pro-inflammatory cytokines and modulates nitric oxide pathways to reduce pain, swelling, and inflammation. Helps strengthen and repair the mucosal lining of the gut and supports nerve regeneration.

Additional Information

Most commonly prescribed for patients with IBS or IBD. From experience with providers, injectable is typically used for soft tissue injuries and inflammation. Capsules are typically prescribed for patients with gut health problems.

TB-500 (Injectable)
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness · Sports Med
About / Clinical Benefits

Thymosin Beta-4. Primarily used to accelerate injury healing, reduce inflammation, and enhance tissue repair in muscles, tendons, and ligaments.

How It Works

Enhances cell migration to injured areas, promotes angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation), and increases actin expression, which is crucial for tissue repair.

Additional Information

There is also Thymosin Alpha-1, which primarily boosts immune function. We currently won't make this but it may come available in the future.

BPC-157 / TB-500 Combo (Injectable)
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness
About / Clinical Benefits

Combination of BPC-157 and TB-500. They work well together as they both reduce inflammation and promote healing and angiogenesis.

How It Works

See BPC-157 and TB-500 entries above.

Additional Information

One of the most popular peptide combinations.

GHK-Cu (Injectable)
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Wellness · Dermatology
About / Clinical Benefits

GHK (glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine) is a naturally occurring tripeptide found in human blood, saliva, and urine. In the body, it readily binds with copper to form GHK-Cu, widely known for promoting tissue repair, collagen production, and cellular regeneration.

How It Works

GHK levels are abundant in young, healthy individuals (around 200 ng/ml at age 20) but plummet by more than half by age 60. Acts as a biochemical messenger that triggers healing responses, regulates the expression of multiple genes, and helps cells revert to a healthier, younger state.

Additional Information

Heavily utilized in dermatology, wellness, and anti-aging therapies. Creams and injectables are blue or have a bluish tint due to the copper. Used in anti-aging topicals to reduce fine lines, increase firmness, and improve elasticity by stimulating collagen and elastin synthesis. Also used in scalp treatments to strengthen hair follicles, minimize shedding, and encourage thicker hair growth. Promotes repair of skin, connective tissue, and blood vessels — used topically for decreased scarring.

GHK-Cu / Epithalon (Injectable)
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Longevity · "Fountain of Youth" formula
About / Clinical Benefits

A popular peptide therapy that combines tissue repair with cellular longevity. Pairs the cosmetic and structural benefits of copper peptides with the DNA-level repair of Epithalon.

How It Works

GHK-Cu — Tissue Regeneration: stimulates skin cells to significantly increase collagen and elastin production. Gene Reprogramming: can influence the expression of over 4,000 genes, reverting aged cells to a healthier state. Anti-Inflammation: limits excess collagen breakdown and calms inflammatory pathways. Epithalon — Telomere Extension: stimulates the enzyme telomerase to extend and maintain telomeres, supporting cellular longevity. Pineal Regulation: stimulates the pineal gland to naturally synthesize melatonin, helping regulate circadian rhythms and restorative sleep.

Additional Information

Epithalon (Epitalon) is a synthetic tetrapeptide that stimulates telomerase activity — the enzyme responsible for maintaining telomere length. We may offer Epithalon by itself as well.

Dihexa (Capsules)
Oral capsules · Cognitive Health · "The Limitless Pill"
About / Clinical Benefits

An investigational, orally active oligopeptide originally developed to treat cognitive decline. Known as the "limitless pill."

How It Works

A nootropic that promotes neuroplasticity by stimulating the formation of new synaptic connections and repairing neurons. An angiotensin IV analog that binds with high affinity to the hepatocyte growth factor (HGF). By mimicking and potentiating HGF, it activates c-Met receptors in the brain, triggering cellular pathways responsible for cognitive function and neural repair.

Additional Information

This was not a huge mover a couple years ago, but with the increased popularity of peptides it will likely be more popular.

Melanotan II (Injectable)
Injectable · Wellness · Urology · Up for Category 2 review July 2026
About / Clinical Benefits

A synthetic peptide designed to mimic the body's natural alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone. It stimulates melanin production to cause skin tanning with minimal UV exposure.

How It Works

Originally developed in the 1980s as a potential skin cancer preventative, MT-II mimics alpha-MSH. Forces the body's melanocytes to produce higher levels of eumelanin, resulting in a dark tan even with minimal UV light.

Additional Information

This one is up for approval in July but it does have some reported side effects so it may not come off the Category 2 list. Unlike standard ED medications which work locally on blood flow, Melanotan II acts on the central nervous system — a non-selective melanocortin receptor agonist (primarily targeting MC4 receptors) that triggers both increased sexual desire and spontaneous erections.

MOTS-C (Injectable)
Injectable · Longevity · Performance
About / Clinical Benefits

A naturally occurring 16-amino acid peptide encoded directly by the mitochondrial DNA. Functions as a systemic signaling hormone that regulates cellular metabolism, promotes physical performance, and improves insulin sensitivity.

How It Works

Energy Regulation: inhibits the folate cycle, which activates AMPK (AMP-activated protein kinase) — a master regulator of energy that signals cells to increase glucose uptake and fat burning. Metabolic Flexibility: targets skeletal muscle to improve metabolic homeostasis. Endogenous levels of MOTS-c increase during physical stress and exercise.

Additional Information

This product is hit or miss. If a clinic specializes in longevity they will most likely order it.

IGF-1 LR3 (Injectable)
Injectable · Muscle · Recovery · Safer alternative to HGH
About / Clinical Benefits

IGF-1 LR3 (Long Arginine 3-Insulin-like Growth Factor 1) is a synthetic, modified analog of human IGF-1. Engineered for enhanced stability with a half-life of 20–30 hours and significantly more potent than naturally occurring IGF-1. Primarily sought for muscle growth, tissue repair, and accelerated recovery.

How It Works

Extended Half-Life: native IGF-1 deactivates in the body within minutes, but IGF-1 LR3's modified structure prevents binding to proteins that would otherwise neutralize it. Hyperplasia: unlike standard hypertrophy, IGF-1 LR3 can promote hyperplasia — the formation of entirely new muscle cells. Nutrient Partitioning: helps direct glucose and amino acids directly into muscle tissue, promoting lean body mass while supporting fat loss.

Additional Information

Because it promotes rapid cellular growth, there are theoretical risks of accelerating the growth of existing cancerous tumors or causing organ enlargement with long-term, unregulated use.

Sermorelin / Ipamorelin (Injectable)
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Wellness · Popular combination
About / Clinical Benefits

A popular peptide therapy used in anti-aging and regenerative medicine to naturally stimulate the pituitary gland into producing and releasing HGH. This dual-action stack maximizes results in recovery, vitality, and body composition.

How It Works

Sermorelin acts upstream by mimicking GHRH, which naturally signals the pituitary gland to increase HGH production. Ipamorelin acts downstream as a selective growth hormone secretagogue that mimics the hunger hormone ghrelin. It fine-tunes the release of HGH while avoiding unwanted spikes in cortisol, prolactin, or hunger that other peptides can cause.

This is a popular and commonly prescribed peptide combination.
