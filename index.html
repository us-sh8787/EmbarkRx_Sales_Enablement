Chapter 02 — Session 1
Our Unique Selling Proposition
What we lead with in every provider conversation
Quality
Third-Party Tested. Every Batch.

Potency, sterility, and endotoxin testing on every compounding run. Full QMS: investigation reporting, root cause analysis, corrective action plans. API identity testing on every new lot received. All third-party tested. Most competitors claim quality — we have documented systems to back it up.

Customer Experience
Accountability. Transparency. Fast Response.

Get back to customers quickly — even without a full answer. Acknowledge first, resolve second. This is the #1 complaint providers have about other pharmacies. Proactive communication on any change that could affect delivery or formulary.

Turnaround Time
Goal: 72 Hours or Under

One week is acceptable but not the standard. Many competitors run 2–3 week turnarounds during peak periods. Speed is a genuine, provable differentiator that directly affects clinic cash flow and patient outcomes.

Competitive Pricing
Win the Business First

Long-term: competitive, not cheapest. Early on, be aggressive — lower price beats no business. Over time, our reputation for being easy to work with becomes the deciding factor. Reps have pricing flexibility; bring competitive pricing from accounts to leadership.

Compliance Differentiators — Lead With These
FDA Greenlist Suppliers
We source all API exclusively from FDA Greenlist suppliers. Many pharmacies use FDA-approved vendors that are NOT Greenlist. FDA enforcement is expected to increase — we are already fully compliant. Lead with this.
No Category 2 Products
We do not compound products on the FDA Category 2 list. Competitors doing so expose their provider accounts to serious regulatory risk — extreme fines and potential shutdown.
Visual Inspection
We perform visual inspection on every batch. Not required by USP standards, but we do it anyway. An extra step above what the industry requires.
COA Verification
Every API shipment comes with a Certificate of Analysis from the vendor. We run our own independent ID test to confirm it before any batch goes into production.

Core Products: GLP-1 Weight Loss
Highest-volume product category — know this cold
⚠ Compliance — Read This First

Do NOT reference commercial drug names (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound) when speaking with providers. Our products are not copycat drugs — they are compounded with B12, B6, or Glycine in addition to the active ingredient. Providers must include a written reason for compounding. We source exclusively from FDA Greenlist suppliers. Lead with this fact in every GLP conversation.

Products — in Excel Order
Semaglutide
Generation 1 · Wellness · Med Spas · Family & Internal Medicine
Start: 0.25–0.5mg/week · Max: 2.5mg/week. First major GLP. Works nearly as well as tirzepatide with more GI side effects (mainly nausea). Lower cost. Resistance training and adequate protein are essential to preserve lean mass and bone density.
Tirzepatide
Generation 2 · Wellness · Med Spas · Bariatric · Plastic Surgery
Start: 2.5mg/week · Max: 15mg/week. Superior outcomes, fewer side effects. Dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist. More expensive but the preferred option for most patients who can afford it.
Ondansetron
Anti-nausea add-on · Wellness · Med Spas
Some providers request this to help patients manage nausea when starting a GLP. Not a significant revenue driver — but a good service offering that builds account loyalty and retention.
Dosing Protocol — Titration

GLP drugs use a titration schedule. Patients start at a lower dose and increase monthly until maximum weight loss with fewest side effects is reached. Sometimes patients plateau — the dose is slightly increased to restart progress.

Note: Semaglutide and tirzepatide are technically peptides, but kept separate in training because they are currently the highest-volume products we make.

Greenlist Supplier — Your Sales Talking Point
Use this in conversations: "We source exclusively from FDA Greenlist suppliers. Many pharmacies don't — when the FDA starts enforcing this more strictly, that will be a significant problem for them and their providers."

⚠ Controlled Substances

Products — in Excel Order
Testosterone Cypionate (Men)
200mg/ml · 5ml & 10ml vials · GSO or MCT oil · IM or SubQ · Wellness · Urology · Men's Health
Most common TRT for men. Dosed 3x/week most commonly. GSO is standard; MCT gaining popularity — less inflammatory. Low Clinic Bill margins. Patient Pay at Urology/OBGYN = best margins. Don't go too low to win the business.
Testosterone Cypionate (Women)
20mg/ml · 5ml vials · SubQ · Wellness · OBGYN · Women's Health
Low-dose TRT for women is increasingly common. SubQ with small insulin needle. Increasingly prescribed for libido and energy. Patient Pay accounts offer the best margins.
Cyp/Prop Blend
160mg/40mg/ml · Injectable · Specialty Wellness
Less common but clinics that use it do high volume. Proprionate has fastest half-life, reducing energy dips between injections.
Enanthate
Medium half-life · Specialty Wellness
Not in current plan due to low demand, but some clinics ask. If a clinic does good volume on enanthate, bring it to leadership for a formulary conversation.
Nandrolone Decanoate
200mg/ml · 10ml vials · Anabolic · Wellness · Urology
Prescribed for bodybuilders or patients with severe low muscle mass. Commercially "Deca." Not common but a good offering to have.
Stanozolol
25mg & 50mg capsules · Oral anabolic · Wellness · Urology · Men's Health
Oral anabolic for the bodybuilding crowd. Commercially "Winstrol." Won't be requested often. Don't lead with it — have it available when a clinic asks.
Oxandrolone (Troche)
20mg & 50mg sublingual · Wellness · Urology
Commercially "Anavar." Troche form bypasses liver metabolism. Scored to quarter for flexible dosing. Growing use in low doses for body composition.
Testosterone Cream
Multiple strengths · Topiclick applicator · Wellness · OBGYN
Topical TRT. Less effective than injectable but achieves reasonable levels. 4 clicks = 1 full dose. Caution: skin transfer to children.
Enclomiphene
12.5mg, 25mg, 50mg capsules · NOT a controlled substance · Men's Health · Wellness · Urology
TRT alternative preserving fertility and testicular function. SERM — drives natural testosterone production. Gets most men to 750–900 ng/dL. No commercial equivalent. Growing fast.
Progesterone
50mg, 75mg, 100mg, 150mg SR capsules · HRT · OBGYN · Wellness · Women's Health
Peri/menopausal symptoms, amenorrhea, endometrial protection. Natural micronized has improved safety profiles for sleep and mood. Recurring script — a strong account anchor product.
Thyroid (T3/T4)
Capsules · Porcine or plant-derived · Functional Medicine · Wellness
Compounded T3/T4 for hypothyroidism. Commercial alternative is Armour Thyroid (porcine). We also offer a plant-derived formulation with tailored T3/T4 ratios — this customization is the key value over commercial options.
Key Formulation & Margin Notes

We start with GSO (grape seed oil). MCT is gaining popularity — less inflammatory, better bioavailability, but currently more expensive. May add MCT as an option or switch if cost comes down.

Sexual Health / ED — in Excel Order
Tadalafil
6mg, 12mg, 24mg · Mint chewable tablets or capsules · Urology · Wellness · Men's Health
Daily or as-needed use. 36hr half-life. Low-dose daily has cardiovascular benefits and helps prevent atherosclerosis. Lower margins but easy to make and co-prescribed with nearly everything we sell.
Sildenafil
50mg, 100mg SR capsules & troches · Urology · Wellness
As-needed use, 4–6hr duration. Troches act faster. Avoid nitrates. Max once daily. Low priapism risk. Troche vs capsule speed is a clinical differentiation to know.
Trimix
Sterile injectable · Urology primary · Cold chain
Injected directly into corpus cavernosum. Must be refrigerated. Active ingredients: Alprostadil (dilates vessels), Papaverine (vasodilator), Phentolamine (blocks vasoconstriction). For older patients or trauma cases.
Super Trimix
Sterile injectable · Urology · Cold chain
Variation of Trimix with twice the amount of Phentolamine. For patients who need a stronger response than standard Trimix provides.
Quad Mix
Sterile injectable · Urology — severe cases only
Stronger than Trimix with a fourth ingredient: Atropine (inhibits nerve impulses responsible for smooth muscle contraction). For very severe ED. NOT starting on the formulary — but we can make it if a clinic requests it. Super Quad Mix also exists.
Scream Cream
Topical cream/gel · Urology · Wellness
Female arousal — increases blood flow to the clitoris. Sildenafil + low-dose Testosterone. Demand has softened as newer peptides offer similar or better outcomes. Carry it; don't lead with it.

B12
Injectable · Wellness · Weight Loss · IV Therapy

Lipo B
Injectable · Wellness · Weight Loss
Lipotropic injection. MIC/B12 (Methionine, Inositol, Choline). Boosts metabolism, increases energy, helps liver process and eliminate dietary and stored fats.
Lipo C
Injectable · Wellness · Weight Loss
Lipotropic injection. MIC/B12 or B6/L-Carnitine. Supports weight loss, boosts metabolism, increases daily energy. Often paired with GLP programs.
Glutathione
Injectable · Wellness · Longevity
Very strong antioxidant. Popular in longevity clinics and bodybuilding communities. Good add-on for wellness accounts already buying GLPs or TRT.
L-Carnitine
Injectable · Wellness · Performance
Aids faster metabolism and improved exercise performance. Popular in wellness and performance-focused clinics. Often co-prescribed with weight loss programs.

Chapters 11 & 12 — Session 2
Peptides: Market & Full Formulary
The fastest-growing category — 17 products in Excel order
How We Got Here
2 Years Ago
503A compounding pharmacies were the primary legal source of peptides. Regulated, quality-controlled, with proper clinical oversight.
Late 2023
FDA placed 19 popular peptides on the "Category 2" list. 503A pharmacies legally prohibited from compounding them. Pharmacies still making them face extreme fines and potential shutdown if audited.
Today
Black market has exploded. Research-only peptides imported from China — essentially unregulated supplements. 4 out of 5 peptides consumed today are from the black market. Patients do not know what they are injecting.
July 2026 + 2027
RFK Jr. is pushing to reverse this for 14 peptides. FDA review panels start July 2026, then early 2027. Significant growth opportunity for compliant 503A pharmacies positioned to move quickly.
Our compliance positioning is a real differentiator. Competitors making Category 2 peptides are exposing their providers to regulatory risk. Use this in conversations — but educate, don't alarm. Frame it as protecting the provider's practice.
Current Formulary — All 17 Products in Excel Order
PT-141 Nasal Spray
Nasal spray · Urology · Wellness
Bremelanotide. Stimulates sexual desire via CNS — not just blood flow. Activates melanocortin receptors in the brain. Works for both men and women. 1–2 sprays per nostril 45–60 min before activity.
NAD+
Injectable · Longevity · Wellness
Not technically a peptide but grouped here — co-prescribed with peptides. Essential coenzyme for energy (ATP), DNA repair, cell signaling. Natural decline with age makes supplementation popular in longevity clinics.
AOD 9604 (Troche)
Sublingual troche · Weight Loss · Wellness
Modified peptide fragment of HGH. Targets visceral and abdominal fat. Sublingual absorption bypasses digestion and liver metabolism. Injectable form is Category 2 — troche form is currently compoundable.
BPC-157 (Injectable)
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness · Sports Med
One of the most popular peptides. Soft-tissue regeneration: tendon/ligament healing, muscle repair, gut health support. Promotes angiogenesis, reduces inflammation. Strong Ortho market opportunity.
BPC-157 (Capsules)
Oral capsules · GI · Wellness
Same compound, different delivery. Capsules primarily for gut health — IBS, IBD, mucosal repair. Know the distinction: injectable for soft tissue, capsules for gut problems. Providers will ask.
TB-500
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness · Sports Med
Thymosin Beta-4. Accelerates injury healing — enhances cell migration, promotes angiogenesis, increases actin expression. Note: Thymosin Alpha-1 exists (immune function) — won't make initially but may add later.
BPC-157 / TB-500 Combo
Injectable · Ortho · Wellness
One of the most popular peptide combinations. Synergistic — both reduce inflammation and promote healing and angiogenesis. Strong target for orthopedic, sports medicine, and active-lifestyle wellness accounts.
GHK-Cu (Injectable)
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Wellness · Derm
Tripeptide that binds copper. Promotes tissue repair, collagen production, cellular regeneration. Levels plummet by more than half between age 20 and 60. Products have a blue or bluish tint due to the copper.
GHK-Cu / Epithalon Blend
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Longevity
GHK-Cu (tissue repair, collagen, gene reprogramming) + Epithalon (stimulates telomerase to extend telomeres, supports cellular longevity, regulates circadian rhythms via pineal gland). Popular "fountain of youth" formula.
Dihexa (Capsules)
Oral · Cognitive Health · Neurology
Investigational nootropic for cognitive decline. "The limitless pill." Angiotensin IV analog binding HGF receptors — promotes neuroplasticity and new synaptic connections. Growing with peptide awareness.
Melanotan II
Injectable · Wellness · Urology
Mimics alpha-MSH. Stimulates melanin production (skin tanning with minimal UV). Also acts on CNS (MC4 receptors) — triggers sexual desire and spontaneous erections. Up for Category 2 review July 2026; outcome uncertain.
MOTS-C
Injectable · Longevity · Performance
Mitochondrial peptide encoded by mitochondrial DNA. Regulates metabolism, promotes physical performance, improves insulin sensitivity. Inhibits folate cycle, activating AMPK. Best for longevity-focused clinics.
IGF-1 LR3
Injectable · Muscle · Recovery · Wellness
Modified IGF-1 analog with 20–30hr half-life. Promotes muscle hyperplasia and nutrient partitioning into muscle. Safer HGH alternative. Note: theoretical risk of accelerating existing tumor growth — providers should screen patients.
Sermorelin
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Wellness · Weight Management
Synthetic GHRH mimic. Stimulates the pituitary to naturally produce and release HGH. Commonly prescribed. Used for energy, sleep quality, and body composition. Off-label use widespread in anti-aging clinics.
Sermorelin / Ipamorelin Blend
Injectable · Anti-Aging · Wellness
One of the most popular peptide combinations. Sermorelin acts upstream (GHRH mimic). Ipamorelin acts downstream as a selective GH secretagogue — mimics ghrelin, fine-tunes HGH release without spiking cortisol, prolactin, or hunger.
GHK-Cu Spray Foam — Hair Restoration
Topical spray foam · Dermatology · Aesthetics · Hair
GHK-Cu as the active ingredient in a spray foam format. Formulation currently in development with a consultant. Large existing market. Strengthens follicles, minimizes shedding, encourages thicker growth.
GHK-Cu Topical Cream — Dermatology
Topical cream · Dermatology · Aesthetics · Anti-Aging
GHK-Cu for skin. Reduces fine lines, increases firmness, improves elasticity by stimulating collagen and elastin. Formulation in development. Large existing market in aesthetics and dermatology accounts.
